Welcome to Alpha-Sci

The Reliable Way to Be Better

Our Purpose

We enable fund managers and equity traders to mature better strategies aimed at outperforming the market. Our software precisely assesses the alpha of a trade in order to find superior alternatives that will gain the fund a better edge over the S&P. Better returns lie in the future, and the key to the future lies in a better understanding of the past.

Our Team

Mexico, Italy, East Coast and West Coast: our team brings together a unique blend of culture and experience that propels our mission forward! Algorithms, securities, programming and finance barely scrape the surface of the nuanced and fascinating challenges our team tackles with hard work and creativity.
